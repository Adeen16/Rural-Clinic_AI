## Frequently asked questions

### How much time will I actually save?

Most users save 20+ hours per month. If you type 2-4 hours a day, Glaido cuts that to 30-60 minutes. That adds up to 1.5-3.5 hours back every single day.

### Will this work with my apps?

Yes. Glaido works in every text field: Gmail, Slack, Notion, VS Code, ChatGPT, and everything else. One hotkey, universal compatibility.

### How long until I am faster with voice than typing?

Most people are faster immediately. Within a few days, expect 3-5x speed. Within two weeks, typing long messages will feel painfully slow.

### How will Glaido help me write code?

For manual coding, you use your keyboard. For agentic coding, Glaido lets you dictate prompts and instructions 5x faster, so you ship more code in less time.

### What makes Glaido different from other voice tools?

At its core, Glaido offers ultra-fast dictation with privacy-first design. On top of that, you get optional AI layers for formatting, prompts, and tool integrations, all fully in your control. Other apps decide for you. Glaido lets you decide.

### Can I dictate in my own language?

Yes. Glaido supports 100+ languages and dialects. Dictate in your native language, in any application.

### Is my data private?

Yes. As developers ourselves, we prioritize privacy. We use open source models, so your data is never shared with big labs. We do not store your dictation data in our database. Only a temporary local history is kept on your device.

### Which operating systems are supported?

Glaido is currently available on macOS only.
